# For Companies

The student association represents students in Data Science and related study
programs at NMBU. We connect students and companies through presentations,
sponsorships, technical events, and visibility.

<PackageGrid />

## Company presentation

Companies get a direct opportunity to meet students and present who they are,
what they work on, their services, technology, culture, and career
opportunities.

After the presentation, there can be food and informal mingling so students can
ask questions and get to know the company in a low-threshold setting.

## Technical event

Companies can host courses, workshops, or technical sessions for students. The
topic should be relevant to Data Science, technology, analytics, AI, software,
or related fields.

The company may briefly introduce itself, but the events should primarily create
value for students through technical content and practical learning.

## Other visibility

We can help promote events, job postings, internships, and other relevant
opportunities for our students.

Get in touch for tailored visibility or collaboration ideas that fit both the
company and the student community.
