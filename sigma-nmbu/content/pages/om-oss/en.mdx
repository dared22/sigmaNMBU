## system_notes

Sigma NMBU is designed as an open training environment for students who want to
learn, experiment, and share experience across courses and cohorts.

We combine technical events with community and low-friction participation so
more students can move from curiosity to hands-on practice in a setting that
feels relevant both academically and socially.
